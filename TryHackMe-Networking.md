## 📡 What is Networking?
- A network is a group of connected devices 
  that can communicate
- Devices are identified by **IP addresses** 
  and **MAC addresses**
- Networks can be private (LAN) or public (Internet)

---

## 🖥️ Intro to LAN
- **LAN** = Local Area Network
- Common topologies: Star, Bus, Ring
- **Switch** = connects devices within a LAN
- **Router** = connects different networks together

---

## 📚 OSI Model – 7 Layers

| Layer | Name | Function |
|-------|------|----------|
| 7 | Application | User-facing apps (HTTP, FTP) |
| 6 | Presentation | Data formatting & encryption |
| 5 | Session | Managing connections |
| 4 | Transport | TCP/UDP – reliable delivery |
| 3 | Network | IP addressing & routing |
| 2 | Data Link | MAC addresses & switching |
| 1 | Physical | Cables, signals, hardware |

---

## 💡 Key Takeaway
> The OSI model helps us understand how data 
> travels across a network — each layer has a 
> specific job and passes data to the next layer.
