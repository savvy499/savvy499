**Date:** 09/03/2026 | **Platform:** ThinkCloudly

---

## 📌 What is a SOC?
A Security Operations Center is a team/function that 
monitors and defends an organisation's IT environment 
— **24x7** and is **not optional**.

## 3 Pillars of a SOC
| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| 👥 People | The analysts and team |
| ⚙️ Process | SOPs – Standard Operating Procedures |
| 🖥️ Tech/Tools | The systems and platforms used |

---

## ✅ Core SOC Functions
1. **Monitor**
2. **Detect**
3. **Response**
4. **Learn & Improve**

## 👥 3 Tiers of SOC Analysts

### 🟠 Tier 1 – Triage (Front Line)
- First to see alerts
- Decides: **threat or false positive?**
- Categorises: real / serious

### 🟡 Tier 2 – Investigation
- Receives escalated alerts
- Deeper analysis
- Uses **Sandbox** to examine safely

### 🟢 Tier 3 – Threat Hunting (Apex)
- **Proactive defenders**
- Actively hunting threats

---

## 💡 Key Takeaway
> SOC is not optional — organisations typically need 
> 10,000+ events monitored, hence the 24x7 model.
